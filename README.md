## 🏏 Assignment 01: International T20 Cricket EDA
**Folder:** `/Assignment-01`

A comprehensive analysis of international T20 cricket matches, focusing on match outcomes, gender-based trends, and automated scorecard generation.

* **Key Features:**
    * Identified top global venues and frequent head-to-head team matchups.
    * Performed data cleaning on nested JSON-like structures within the dataset.
    * **Scorecard Generator:** A custom-built function that extracts delivery-level data to create a detailed side-by-side batting and bowling summary.
* **Visualizations:** * **Histogram:** Distribution of win margins by runs.
    * **Box Plot:** Comparing win margins between Male and Female T20 matches.
    * **Heatmap:** Correlation between match metrics.
* **Numpy Operations:** Array transposing, reshaping, and broadcasting.

## 🎬 Assignment 02: IMDB Movie Industry Analysis
**Folder:** `/Assignment-02`

An exploratory data analysis of 3,000 movies designed to provide strategic production and casting recommendations for a rookie movie producer.

* **Business Insights:**
    * Identified the highest profit-making films and their respective creative teams (Directors/Producers).
    * Calculated **Return on Investment (ROI)** across different languages and producer portfolios.
    * Performed a "Deep Dive" into actor careers to identify the most frequent and profitable cast members.
    * Analyzed director-actor partnerships to find preferred casting patterns.
* **NumPy Mastery:** Includes exact implementations of 3D tensor reshaping, axis-based insertion/deletion, broadcasting, and flattening.
* **EDA Visualizations:**
    * **Correlation Heatmap:** Identifying relationships between budget, popularity, and revenue.
    * **Box Plots:** Visualizing profit distribution and outliers across major genres using logarithmic scales.
    * **Histogram:** Distribution of movie runtimes.
